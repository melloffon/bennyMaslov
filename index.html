About

Benny was born in 1977 Chelyabinsk, USSR. At the age of 9 he took up Ballroom Dancing and proceeded to compete internationally taking part in numerous international competitions and World Latin and Ballroom Dance Championships through his teenage years. Benny has moved to Israel where he graduated high school and studies interior design and architecture and then moved to UK where he graduated Central School of Ballet and holds a Diploma in Classical Ballet and related subjects. He had an extensive career in Dance, Theatre, Film and TV and is now venturing into his own projects alongside the stage and screen. Benny has written a libretto for a ballet and a libretto for an opera in collaboration with composer Oleg Plotnikov. Benny delivers a variety of workshops for various technical aspects of dance, yoga, fitness and mental health in collaboration with mental health professionals such as world champion free diver Marina Kazankova. He is a cofounder of www.wisdomofdance.com which provides workshops on transferable skills to aid relationships and improve all areas of life.
